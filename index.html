Free and accessible leadership development programs for youth; delivered through small-group coaching, mentorship, and experiential learning.

Core Track

GYM Foundational Program

A free, three-month leadership and life skills program for high school students, offered twice annually through in-person and virtual weekend sessions.

Ongoing

Greatness Club

An ongoing free leadership development and service program for program graduates, focused on communication, mentorship, and community impact.

We offer our GYM Foundational program free twice a year for high school students. Classes are held weekly on Sundays, 3–4:30pm at our Phoenix location; students outside the metro area join via Zoom.

When we run

  • Spring — January through April
  • Fall — August through November

If you would like to enroll your student into one of the batches or want to learn more about Leadership GYM, please reach out to us.
